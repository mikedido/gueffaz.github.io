I also worked in ScaleSem project during my PhD. ScaleSem is located in the Back-Office layer of the CheckSem architecture, which forms the basis of the CheckSem model. This part, non visible users of the system, offers functions to extract the knowledge contained in all kinds of structures or to translate knowledge of a given semantic formalism to another.
